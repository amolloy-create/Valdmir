Introduction

The Rubik's Cube is a 3D combination puzzle that has fascinated people for decades. In this guide, we'll break down the process of solving it step by step. Understanding basic notation is essential before getting started.

Basic Notation

  • U (Up): Turn the upper face 90 degrees clockwise.
  • D (Down): Turn the bottom face 90 degrees clockwise.
  • F (Front): Turn the front face 90 degrees clockwise.
  • B (Back): Turn the back face 90 degrees clockwise.
  • R (Right): Turn the right face 90 degrees clockwise.
  • L (Left): Turn the left face 90 degrees clockwise.

For each move, you can turn the face 90 degrees, 180 degrees, or 270 degrees in either direction.

Step 1: Solve the First Layer

The first layer involves solving the white cross and positioning the white corners in the correct spot.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Step 1: Solve the white cross (place the white edge pieces around the white center piece).
  • Step 2: Position the white corner pieces to form the first layer, ensuring that the edge pieces match the adjacent center pieces.

Step 2 & 3: Solve the Second and Third Layers

After solving the first layer, the next steps involve completing the second and third layers.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Step 1: Solve the second layer by positioning the edge pieces in the correct spots without disturbing the first layer.
  • Step 2: Finally, solve the third layer by positioning the remaining edge and corner pieces to complete the cube.
